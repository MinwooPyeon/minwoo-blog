## 핵심 답변 구조 — 3단 구성

모든 OPIc 답변은 다음 구조로 말하면 일관성과 점수가 올라갑니다.

```
① 두berliner (Two-liner) — 핵심 답변 먼저
② 근거·세부 내용 — 이유, 묘사, 에피소드
③ 마무리 — 느낀 점, 현재와 연결
```

**예시 — "What kind of music do you listen to?"**

```
① I'm really into K-pop and indie music these days.

② I usually listen to music on my way to school or when I'm studying.
   My favorite artist is IU because her voice is very soothing.
   I remember one time I found her new album right before an exam,
   and it actually helped me focus better.

③ Listening to music has become a daily routine for me,
   and I can't imagine my day without it.
```

## 시제별 표현 모음

### 현재 습관 (묘사 유형)

| 상황 | 표현 |
|------|------|
| 자주 ~한다 | I usually / I tend to / I make it a habit to |
| 가끔 ~한다 | I sometimes / Once in a while |
| ~하는 것을 좋아한다 | I enjoy -ing / I'm really into |
| ~하지 않는다 | I rarely / I'm not much of a + 명사 |

### 과거 경험 (경험 유형)

| 상황 | 표현 |
|------|------|
| ~하던 때가 있었다 | There was a time when / I remember when |
| 그 당시에 | At that time / Back then |
| ~해서 당황했다 | I was caught off guard / I was a bit flustered |
| ~으로 해결했다 | I managed to / I ended up -ing |

### 감정·마무리

| 상황 | 표현 |
|------|------|
| 좋은 경험이었다 | It was a great / memorable experience |
| 많이 배웠다 | I learned a lot from it / It was quite eye-opening |
| 다시 하고 싶다 | I'd love to do it again |

## 빈출 주제별 필수 내용

### 주제 1 — 집/거주지

**묘사 문제:** "Describe your home."

준비할 내용:
- 집 위치, 규모 (아파트 몇 층, 방 수)
- 좋아하는 공간 + 이유
- 최근 있었던 일 (에피소드)

필수 표현:
```
I live in an apartment on the 10th floor.
My favorite spot is my room because it's quiet and I can focus.
I usually spend most of my time at my desk, studying or coding.
```

---

### 주제 2 — 여가 활동 (음악/영화)

**경험 문제:** "Tell me about a movie you recently watched."

준비할 내용:
- 영화 제목, 장르
- 인상 깊었던 장면 + 이유
- 함께 본 사람, 관람 후 느낌

필수 표현:
```
I watched [영화제목] a while ago with my friend.
The scene that stood out the most was when...
I was totally hooked from the beginning.
It made me think about...
```

---

### 주제 3 — 여행

**경험 문제:** "Tell me about a trip you've taken."

준비할 내용:
- 여행지, 동행, 기간
- 기억에 남는 에피소드 (문제 상황 포함)
- 여행 후 소감

필수 표현:
```
I went to [장소] with [동행] last [시기].
We had a minor issue when [문제 상황].
Fortunately, we managed to [해결 방법].
Overall, it was one of the best trips I've ever taken.
```

---

### 주제 4 — 건강/운동

**묘사 + 경험 혼합:** "What do you do to stay healthy?"

준비할 내용:
- 주로 하는 운동 종류
- 운동 루틴 (언제, 얼마나)
- 효과나 변화

필수 표현:
```
I try to work out at least three times a week.
I go jogging near my apartment in the morning.
Since I started exercising regularly, I've been feeling much more energized.
```

## 돌발 문제 대처 전략

예상치 못한 주제가 나왔을 때 당황하지 않는 방법입니다.

### 전략 1 — 공통 에피소드 뼈대 활용

어떤 주제든 아래 틀로 시작합니다:

```
"I have a memory related to this topic."
"Let me tell you about an experience I had."
```

이후 육하원칙(When, Where, Who, What, Why, How)으로 채웁니다.

### 전략 2 — 시간 버는 표현

```
"That's a great question. Let me think for a second."
"Well, when it comes to this topic..."
"Honestly speaking..."
"If I think about it..."
```

### 전략 3 — 모르는 단어는 돌려 말하기

단어가 생각나지 않을 때:
```
"A device that you use to... / A person who..."
"I'm not sure what it's called in English, but..."
"It's something like..."
```

## 롤플레이 유형 공략

롤플레이는 3문장(질문 3개 or 문제 해결) 구조로 접근합니다.

**질문 3개 유형 예시:**

> "Call a hotel and ask 3 questions about your reservation."

```
Hello, I'd like to ask a few questions about my reservation.

First, I'd like to confirm the check-in time.
Second, I was wondering if breakfast is included.
And lastly, is there a parking lot available nearby?

I'd really appreciate your help. Thank you.
```

**문제 해결 유형 예시:**

> "You rented a car but it broke down. Call and explain the situation."

```
Hi, I'm calling because I have a problem with the rental car.

I rented a car from your company this morning, but it suddenly broke down
about 10 minutes ago on the highway near [장소].

I'd like to know if you can send someone to help me right away.
Also, could you arrange an alternative vehicle for me?

I'm really in a hurry, so I'd appreciate a quick response. Thank you.
```

## 정리

- 모든 답변은 **핵심 → 근거 → 마무리** 3단 구성
- 과거 경험 유형이 배점 핵심 — 3~4개 스토리를 충분히 준비
- 돌발 문제엔 시간 버는 표현 + 육하원칙으로 대응
- 롤플레이는 질문 3개 or 문제 해결 구조를 암기
